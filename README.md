<h1 style="font-size:80px; text-align: Center; color:#Ff0000; font-style: Verdana;">Who we are</h1>
<p1 style=font-size:80px;"> The Labour Party (LP) is a social democratic political party in Nigeria. The party was created in 2002 and was previously known as the Party for Social Democracy (PSD) before changing its name to the Labour Party. Built on the ideology of social democracy, the party aims to promote and defend social democratic principles and ideals for the purpose of achieving social justice, progress and unity </p>

<h2 style="font-size:80px; Color:#008000; text-align:Center">Our History</h2>
  <p>The Party was formed in 2002, as the Party for Social Democracy before its name was officially changed to the Labour Party after the 2003 general election.<br>After the late National Chairman, Alhaji Abdul""kadir Abdulsalam, died in 2020,the party has been going through a serious leadership crisis. Barrister Julius Abure who was the National Secretary of Labour Party was elected as the new National Chairman to replace late Alhaji Abdulkadir Abdulsalam by the Labour Party National Executive Council (NEC) in 2021. <br>The former Deputy National Chairman of the party, Calistus Okafor challenged Barrister Julius Abure after he claimed to be the authentic National Chairman of the party by virtue ug of his position.</p>
  <h3 style="font-Size:80px;color:#008000;text-align:Center">Our Party Logo</h3>
  <img src="https://encrypted-tbn0.gstatic.com/images?q=tbn:ANd9GcQlZKJrjHiXkgUxa9vpS3dNgY1OLkYm0HhDIg&usqp=CAU; alt="Labour Party Logo" Width="800" height="800"/>
  <p>The logo of the party is a wheel with a man and woman with a child engraved in the centre.<br>The wheel stands for industry and work as basis for economic empowerment of the populace and the prosperity of the nation, i.e. continuous economic growth and development.<br> The linkage, between the wheel and man, woman and child connotes that governance, economic and social development must lead to the advancement of human beings.  </p>
  <h4 style="font-size:80px; text-align: center; color:#008000">Our Party Flag</h4>
